About FOCUS

FOCUS is a privacy-first production management hub for content creation teams. It helps blind and sighted creators organize projects, scripts, resources, roles, notes, and handoffs without requiring legal names, email addresses, or passwords.

What FOCUS is for

FOCUS is designed for small creative groups that need a shared place to track video production work. A group can create projects, assign collaborators, add resource links, leave notes, invite members, and keep track of project status.

How accounts work

The app is built around pseudonymous accounts. People can choose a display name, add a short bio, and decide whether assigned projects appear on their member profile. Sign-in is intended to use account providers and passkeys instead of collecting passwords or email addresses.

Current state

FOCUS is in active development and is not production-ready yet. The current app supports local development workflows, passkeys, backup keys, production groups, invitations, project tracking, project notes, project exports, deletion, notifications, and accessibility-focused templates.
